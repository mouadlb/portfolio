My Process

Discovery

I begin by deeply understanding the problem space through user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to establish a solid foundation.

Ideation

Here I explore multiple directions through sketches, wireframes, and collaborative workshops to identify the most promising solutions.

Refinement

I craft high-fidelity designs and interactive prototypes, paying meticulous attention to visual details, interactions, and accessibility.

Testing

I validate designs through usability testing, gathering feedback from real users to identify pain points and opportunities for improvement.

Iteration

Based on user feedback and metrics, I iteratively refine the design, addressing pain points and enhancing successful elements.

Implementation

I collaborate closely with developers to ensure the design vision is faithfully executed, providing specifications and support throughout development.
